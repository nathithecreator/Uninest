About   Uninest Go

UniNest Go is a purpose-built student accommodation platform designed to simplify the search for safe, affordable housing near universities. We understand the unique challenges students face—especially those relocating from other cities or countries—such as unfamiliar neighborhoods, limited trusted listings, and the risk of scams. Our solution brings peace of mind through verified listings, peer reviews, and a student-only community, all in one user-friendly mobile app.

More than just a property search tool, UniNest Go is a comprehensive student living companion. From digital lease signing and maintenance requests to roommate matching, local guides, and exclusive student deals, we support every step of your journey. Our mission is to make settling into student life easier, safer, and more connected—so students can focus less on housing stress and more on their future.

Empowering Landlords to Reach the Right Tenants with   Uninest Go

UniNest Go isn’t just built for students—it’s also a powerful platform for landlords looking to connect with a steady, reliable market of student tenants. By listing on UniNest Go, landlords gain access to a large pool of verified students actively searching for accommodation near their universities. Our platform ensures that listings stand out through targeted visibility, peer reviews, and verified property badges—building credibility and helping landlords attract serious applicants. With digital lease signing, application management, and in-app communication tools, landlords can streamline the entire rental process while maintaining control and transparency.
